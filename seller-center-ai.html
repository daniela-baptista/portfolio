Seller Center + AI Chatbot

Product strategy initiative designed to centralise seller support and scale service operations through AI automation.

Chatbot / Seller Center visual coming soon

Problem

Seller support was highly fragmented across different touchpoints, with a growing volume of requests and limited scalability. This created slower response times, operational pressure and a less autonomous seller experience.

Action

Developed the concept and strategic pitch for a centralised Seller Center support experience integrated with an AI chatbot. The objective was to create a single support ecosystem where sellers could access information, track requests and resolve common issues faster.

My role

Defined the business problem and translated operational pain points into a product opportunity. Built the strategic pitch and use case for the chatbot solution. Mapped how the chatbot could support the seller journey inside the platform and reduce dependency on manual support.

Impact

Expected impact included faster issue resolution, lower support dependency and improved seller satisfaction through clearer self-service flows. The initiative also supported long-term operational scalability by reducing manual workload and enabling a more proactive support model.
