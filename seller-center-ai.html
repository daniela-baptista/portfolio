The chatbot born from a business case

An AI-enabled support solution designed to reduce repetitive seller contacts, improve self-service and scale operational efficiency.

Business Problem

Seller support teams were handling a high volume of repetitive requests, many related to order status, onboarding questions and platform usage.

The challenge was to reduce dependency on human intervention without compromising trust.

Approach

I mapped the most frequent support journeys, identified automation opportunities and structured the business case for an AI chatbot embedded in Seller Center.

The focus was on first-contact resolution for low-complexity requests and intelligent routing for escalations.

Before vs After

Before

Sellers relied heavily on support agents for routine questions, increasing response time and team workload.

After

AI-driven conversational guidance enabled instant answers, reduced tickets and improved autonomy.

Product Thinking

Beyond the chatbot itself, the project involved defining intents, fallback logic, escalation pathways and content governance.

This was positioned as a scalable product layer rather than a one-off support feature.
