Seller Center + AI Chatbot

Improving seller autonomy through centralized support and AI-powered assistance.

Key Insight

High-volume repetitive support contacts were driven by information fragmentation

Sellers lacked a centralized experience to quickly resolve operational questions.

The Problem

Support teams were receiving repetitive queries that could be solved through self-service and automated guidance.

My Approach

  • Mapped seller support pain points
  • Identified repetitive ticket categories
  • Designed centralized support journey
  • Proposed AI chatbot flows and escalation logic

Impact

Increased seller autonomy and reduced repetitive contacts through faster access to support information and AI-guided journeys.
