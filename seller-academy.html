The problem

A large share of seller support tickets came from recurring operational questions: product upload, commissions, orders, returns and payments.

The knowledge existed, but was dispersed across emails, documents and team memory, creating unnecessary support dependency.

What was built

Defined the information architecture and navigation logic based on the real seller journey: onboarding → product upload → order management → payments → support.

Built content structures, editorial guidelines and webinar-based educational content for recurring seller pain points.
