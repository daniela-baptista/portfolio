Seller Academy enablement journey

A learning and enablement initiative designed to improve seller onboarding, platform adoption and operational autonomy.

Challenge

Sellers lacked structured guidance on how to use the platform effectively, leading to recurring support requests and slower time-to-value.

The challenge was to design an enablement journey that reduced friction and increased confidence from day one.

Key Focus Areas

Onboard

new seller activation

Learn

platform education flows

Adopt

self-service enablement

Learning Journey

1. Welcome & Setup

First-touch guidance and key actions to get started.

2. Operational Training

Inventory, order handling and customer support best practices.

3. Growth & Optimisation

Performance insights and continuous enablement resources.

Before vs After

Before

Fragmented knowledge, inconsistent onboarding and high reliance on support teams.

After

Structured learning journey enabling faster autonomy and better adoption.

Strategic Impact

This initiative repositioned seller education as a product layer that directly supports operational efficiency and long-term retention.
