Transactional communication redesign

A customer journey optimisation project focused on improving transactional email clarity, trust and actionability across key touchpoints.

Problem Statement

Customers were receiving transactional emails that lacked clarity, created confusion around order status and contributed to avoidable contacts.

The objective was to redesign the communication flow to better support decision-making and reduce friction.

Email Journey Redesign

1. Order Confirmation

Improved visibility over next steps and expectations.

2. Shipping Update

Clear ETA, milestone visibility and reassurance messaging.

3. Delivery / Post-Purchase

Confirmation, support pathways and follow-up guidance.

Before vs After

Before

Generic messaging, low context and unclear actions led to repeated questions.

After

Action-oriented content and better journey sequencing improved trust and reduced ambiguity.

Strategic Value

This project repositioned transactional emails as a productised customer journey layer rather than isolated communication assets.
