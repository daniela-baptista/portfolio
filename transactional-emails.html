Transactional Communication Redesign

Post-purchase emails were creating more confusion than clarity. A full redesign of the customer and seller communication journey focused on transparency, consistency with checkout and reducing avoidable support contacts.

Problem

Post-purchase emails lacked consistency across touchpoints. Customers received different date formats, ambiguous phrasing and messaging disconnected from what they had seen at checkout.

For sellers, email logic was opaque and disconnected from the platform experience, generating unnecessary support contacts on both sides.

How it was built

Customer emails
Redesigned order confirmation, shipping and non-acceptance emails with consistent date formatting, clearer status language and aligned messaging with checkout.

Delivery
Each email was analysed for hierarchy, tone and platform alignment. HTML was written and tested in DEV for PT and ES. All changes were tracked in Jira and documented in Confluence.
