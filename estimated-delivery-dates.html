The Problem

Customer dissatisfaction around delivery timelines was one of the main drivers of post-purchase support contacts and negative feedback.

Initial assumptions pointed toward logistics delays, but deeper analysis revealed a communication and expectation gap.

My Approach

  • Analysed 346,000 shipment records
  • Reviewed complaint themes and support tickets
  • Cross-checked SLA vs perceived delay
  • Mapped customer expectation touchpoints
  • Identified ETA communication gaps

Solution Design

I proposed improvements to the estimated delivery communication flow, focusing on expectation setting and timeline clarity.

Before

Generic delivery windows and limited visibility created the perception of delay.

After

Clearer ETA messaging and improved milestone visibility reduced confusion and repetitive support contacts.

Business Impact

This project shifted the focus from operational blame to expectation management, creating the basis for reducing support contacts and improving customer trust.
