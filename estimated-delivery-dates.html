Estimated Delivery Dates

Customer journey initiative focused on improving delivery expectation clarity and reducing dissatisfaction.

Delivery flow mockup coming soon

Problem

Customers had limited visibility regarding actual delivery timing, creating false expectations and dissatisfaction.

Action

Implemented estimated delivery dates across PDP, checkout and transactional emails.

My role

Led the initial analysis and helped define the customer communication logic with cross-functional teams.

Impact

Improved expectation management and contributed to higher satisfaction and reduced post-purchase friction.
