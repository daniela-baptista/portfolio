Transactional Emails Redesign

Customer communication initiative focused on improving clarity, trust and post-purchase experience through redesigned transactional emails.

Email journey / communication flow visual coming soon

Problem

Post-purchase communication lacked clarity and consistency, creating confusion around order status, delivery expectations and next steps in the customer journey.

Action

Redesigned the transactional email journey to provide clearer order status updates, estimated delivery information and more consistent messaging across all touchpoints.

My role

Contributed to journey analysis, communication logic, information hierarchy and cross-functional alignment with customer, platform and brand teams.

Impact

Improved communication transparency and reduced uncertainty after purchase, supporting customer trust, satisfaction and a smoother post-purchase experience.
