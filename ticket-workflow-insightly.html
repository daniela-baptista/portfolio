Turning support operations into a scalable system

Seller support was operational, but knowledge lived across shared folders, Word files, Excel trackers and email threads. The challenge was to centralise processes, create visibility and build a scalable ticket workflow accessible to the whole team.

Problem

Support processes existed, but they were fragmented across inboxes, shared folders, Word documents and Excel trackers.

This created dependency on tacit knowledge, limited visibility into issue volume and resolution times, and made onboarding new team members difficult.

What was built

Designed and implemented a structured ticket workflow inside Insightly, including issue typologies, field dependencies, routing logic and SLA tracking.

Operational knowledge was migrated into Confluence to create a central, accessible documentation layer for the full support team.
